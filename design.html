This Site

I mean, look at it. A definition of who I am and my stylistic preferences. Meanwhile, I balance usability and interactivity into various elements, such as hover effects and HTML development. Don't even get me started on the simplicity of the site. How much data is the entire site? 1.21MB. Yes, you read that right. This entire site, which is extremely professional, and aesthetically pleasing, is fast enough to load every single webpage on this site in just about a second, on 3G internet. The images and your ability to read are the only parts slowing down your experience. If I can cram this much into a site so small, imagine what I could do with a much higher amount of resources and constant information to share.

TechPoint Xtern Submission

In the fall of 2023, I applied for TechPoint's Xtern program, which aims to connect aspiring youth in college with nearby companies to build the local tech industry while promoting youth, service, and community engagement. I had passed the first step of Xtern's application, and was instructed to create deliverables for a work assessment. The topic was FoodX, a theoretical food promotion program that helped promote tech, food, and culture in Indianapolis. To the right and at the link below, you can find a sample of my work.

Photography

For all my life, I have had an eye for photography. The art of capturing the human experience, and the tools we use to achieve greater, faster, and more things is fascinating. How much story can you fit into one image? It's a puzzle, and yet expression at the same time. However, in fall 2023, I began to work with a mini-film camera and my phone's camera to experiment. I realized one of the more forgotten careers in the digital content scene is photographers. We see hundreds, if not thousands of photographs a day, with each telling unique stories, being processed and edited. One of my photos is to the left, and you can see more of my work below.
